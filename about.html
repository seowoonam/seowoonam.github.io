Seowoo Nam is a design researcher interested in new forms of agency enabled by data. Exploring alternative modes of data collection and sensemaking, she develops posthumanist design approaches for intelligent agents and their entanglements. Her research centers on human-AI interaction: how data-driven systems co-evolve their own perception, agencies and trajectories with people. It includes questions such as how things gather contextual data for design ethnography and how environmental data can help gain a new understanding of more-than-humans.
