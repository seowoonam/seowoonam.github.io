Seowoo is a design researcher who explores emergent, entangled, situated, and relational aspects of technological mediation. In particular, she is interested in how designed artefacts as an experiential and tangible materialisation of technology can help address socio-technical issues.

Hovering around and sometimes grounded in theories of posthuman and complexity, she enjoys bridging theories and situated knowledge through practices of Research through Design (RtD). By thinking and tinkering with technology, she brings alternative ontologies and epistemologies down to earth.

Her current interest areas include 1) more-than-human design approaches/strategies for intelligent agents and data-driven systems and 2) alternative modes of data collection and sensemaking.
