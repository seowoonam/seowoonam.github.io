Seowoo is a design researcher who explores emergent, entangled, situated, and relational aspects of human-algorithm interaction. In particular, she is interested in alternative ways of collecting and sensemaking data for sustainable, inclusive, and resilient futures.

Hovering around and sometimes grounded in theories of posthuman and complexity, she enjoys bridging theories and situated knowledge through practices of Research through Design (RtD). By thinking and tinkering with technology, she brings alternative ontologies and epistemologies down to earth.

Her current research investigates predictive systems - algorithmic ways of inferring the future to better understand the present. By critically examining how computational models can shape us and our reality, she explores how more-than-human granularity can open up new modes of becoming and knowing.
