PAY OR CARE

Seowoo Nam is a design researcher interested in new forms of agency enabled by data. Exploring alternative modes of data collection and sensemaking, she develops design strategies for intelligent agents and infrastructures. Her research centers on human-algorithm interaction: how data-driven systems co-evolve their agencies with people and how they exert their own agencies. It includes questions such as 1) how things gather contextual data for design ethnography and 2) how environmental data can help gain a new understanding of more-than-humans.
